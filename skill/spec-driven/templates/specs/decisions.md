# Decisions

> A running log of the choices you've made and — most importantly — the options you
> looked at and **rejected, and why.** This is the single most valuable file in the kit:
> it's what stops an AI assistant from cheerfully re-suggesting an approach your team
> already tried and threw out. Each entry is one decision. (These are sometimes called
> "ADRs," short for Architecture Decision Records — but you don't need the jargon to use them.)
